Her Story

From the Stage to the Strategy Room

Braela Banks has never been the type to sit on the sidelines. Born and raised in Atlanta, she grew up surrounded by culture, ambition, and a city that rewards people who move with intention. From the pageant stage to boardrooms, Braela learned early that confidence is a currency — and she's been investing it ever since.

As Miss Buckhead USA 2024, Braela represented more than a title. She carried a platform rooted in purpose — using her visibility to advocate against human trafficking and shine a light on the fight that too often stays in the shadows. That same energy — fierce, purposeful, unapologetic — is exactly what she brings to every client she works with.

Digital Bee Marketing was born from a simple observation: too many talented business owners and creatives were being overlooked because they didn't have the right marketing behind them. Braela set out to change that. She built Digital Bee to be the marketing partner she wished she had — one that moves fast, thinks creatively, and treats every client's brand like it matters. Because it does.
